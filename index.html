OUR MEDICAL

We’re Setting the Standards in Research & Clinical Care

Formally known as Hope Maternity and Nursing Home, Hope Medical Center (HMC) was founded in early 2010 as a local solution to a community need for quality care. The idea of a health centre at the heart of the village was the result of interactions with locals, at the Hope Chemist, who turned to the community pharmacy for answers to health questions they couldn’t answer themselves. This was because there was no reliable clinic in the vicinity of Awasi Township. .
